AI has created a new production question for every marketing team.

Unfortunately, it is usually the wrong first question.

Before asking whether a campaign should use AI, traditional production or a hybrid workflow, ask what the campaign must make the audience notice, feel or do.

Once that job is clear, the production method has to earn its place.

AI may give the idea scale, speed or variation. A real performance may create the trust the message needs. A hybrid route may protect what must be authentic while extending what can be imagined.

The smartest method is not automatically the newest or the most traditional. It is the one that helps the campaign say what matters and spend where it counts.
