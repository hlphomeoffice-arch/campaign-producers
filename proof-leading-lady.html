Proof 03 / Independent film campaign

176,000 admissions from 44 theatres.

Then Leading Lady travelled to 15 territories and Netflix USA.

An independent romantic comedy became South Africa's second-highest-grossing local film of 2014. The campaign turned a limited theatrical footprint into audience demand - and audience demand into international leverage.

The campaign challenge

Independent scale. Studio-sized competition.

The film did not have the footprint of a Hollywood release. It still had to earn national attention, convert curiosity into paid admissions and give international buyers a reason to believe.

01 / Reach

A limited cinema footprint.

Every location had to work harder. The campaign could not rely on ubiquitous placement.

02 / Choice

A crowded release window.

The audience was choosing between a local independent film and heavily marketed studio titles.

03 / Leverage

A bigger ambition than one territory.

The domestic result needed to become commercial evidence for international distribution.

The campaign producer's job

Give every piece of attention somewhere to go.

The work was not simply to advertise a film. It was to build a chain of momentum - from a clear audience promise to opening-weekend relevance, sustained admissions and international sales proof.

  1. 01
    Position

    Make the contrast memorable.

    The film's cross-cultural premise became an immediate, repeatable reason to care.

  2. 02
    Concentrate

    Turn release week into a public event.

    Press, radio, social conversation and audience word of mouth were focused around the theatrical opening.

  3. 03
    Convert

    Let the audience result sell the next opportunity.

    Domestic traction became evidence for theatrical, VOD and international distribution conversations.

The result

Attention became a commercial asset.

The strongest proof is not any one number. It is the sequence: a limited independent release created enough audience movement to rank nationally, travel internationally and reach a major streaming platform.
