Before you begin

Clear answers. No hidden assumptions.

What is included in the monthly retainer?

Every partnership includes audience and campaign strategy, concepts, messaging, copy, scripts, production planning, budget guidance, creative direction and hands-on management. The partnership sets the number of active workstreams and hero assets.

What counts as a hero asset?

A hero asset is the lead piece that carries the campaign. It could be a film, commercial, key visual, launch presentation, landing page, podcast episode or another agreed priority asset.

What does hands-on production management mean?

We turn the campaign idea into a practical brief, choose the production route, coordinate contributors, manage reviews and approvals, and keep the work aligned from first decision to final delivery.

Can you work with our in-house team or existing agency?

Yes. We can lead the campaign and production process while working with your leadership, marketing team, agency, freelancers and specialist suppliers.

Which partnership is right for us?

Choose Campaign Producer for one important campaign and one hero asset each month. Choose Campaign Growth Partner for up to two active workstreams and two hero assets. Choose Embedded Campaign Producer for ongoing leadership and up to four hero assets.

How do you review campaign performance?

We use the evidence available to your team, including response, engagement, conversion and commercial feedback, to see what is working and choose the next priority.
